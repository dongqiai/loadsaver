LoadSaver

Free CBM and container loading calculator.

LoadSaver helps businesses quickly calculate CBM (cubic meters), cargo volume, and container capacity for common freight containers. Enter carton or pallet dimensions and LoadSaver will compute volume, fit, and estimated utilization.

The tool also provides cost-efficiency loading suggestions to help shippers choose the most economical container mix based on volume and utilization.
